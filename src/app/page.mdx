### About Us

We are the **CanX** team, and we are passionate about designing and developing exceptional **FiveM scripts**. Our primary objective is to set a benchmark for excellence in the FiveM scripting community. We are committed to creating scripts that are not only visually appealing but also highly optimized and meticulously clean.

Clean Code: We take pride in our clean and well-organized code, making it easier for other developers to understand, modify, and extend our scripts.

Join us on our journey to redefine what it means to create high-quality FiveM scripts. Together, we can raise the bar and contribute to a thriving FiveM community.
